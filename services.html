Services

Support that moves
and grows with you

Whether you're expecting, newly postpartum, or months into your breastfeeding journey — personalized support is available.

As part of my work to sit for the IBCLC exam, I am currently offering services at no cost. These free services are truly no-strings-attached. When issues fall outside my scope of practice, I will provide referrals to the appropriate providers.

How We Meet

How we meet

Home Visit

Serving Northwest Ohio and Southeast Michigan with home visits. Get hands-on support in the comfort of your own home — I come to you.

Virtual Appointment

Available nationwide & internationally

Available nationwide and internationally. Answers to your urgent questions and real-time support when you need it most.

Prenatal Consultation

Prepare before baby arrives. Available in-home or virtually. I'll cover latch, supply, common challenges, and everything to set you up for success.

Common Reasons to Seek Support

I help with all of this — and more

Painful Latching & Nipple Damage

You shouldn't have to "tough it out." I'll identify the issues, optimize latch and positioning, and find feeding solutions that prioritize the comfort of both you and baby.

Low Milk Supply Concerns

Clinical evaluations and sustainable milk-boosting strategies tailored to your body's needs. Whether you need a boost or a balanced plan for supplementation, I provide data-driven strategies to help you reach your goals.

Pumping & Flange Sizing

The right flange size can make a world of difference. Pain subsides and output increases. Learn how to pump responsively and how to get your best output in less time.

Return to Work

Get equipped, gain strategy and feel confident in your pumping. Practical pumping logistics and sustainable back-to-work plans that prioritize your well-being.

Formula Feeding & Combo Feeding

Objective, judgement-free guidance on formula selection and combo-feeding transitions, including bottle-feeding techniques for a happy, healthy baby.

Feeling Unsure

From storage guidelines to reflux to nighttime feeds to pacifiers — there are many moments of "am I doing this right?" I'm here to cut through the noise and provide you with the confidence to keep moving forward.
