Quick Answers

Frequently asked questions

How long is a consultation?

Initial consultations are typically 60–90 minutes. Follow-up sessions are usually 45–60 minutes. I never rush — we take the time your situation needs.

Is it really free?

Yes! I'm currently working toward my IBCLC certification and offering services at no cost. There are absolutely no strings attached — this is real, compassionate care.

How quickly can I get an appointment?

I understand breastfeeding concerns can't always wait, and I do my best to see families as soon as possible.

What if my issue is outside your scope of practice?

When issues fall outside my scope, I will always provide referrals to the appropriate providers — including physicians, pediatricians, and specialists.

Do you support formula and combo feeding?

Absolutely. My support is judgment-free. However you choose to feed your baby, I'm here to help make it work for your family.
