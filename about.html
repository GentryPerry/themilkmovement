Sierra Perry, Certified Breastfeeding Specialist

I believe every feeding journey is special and is worthy of supporting.

If you're feeling overwhelmed, confused by conflicting advice, or simply just need reassurance - I'm ready to help you find joy, comfort and confidence in feeding your baby. Together, we'll create a personalized plan that works for you and your family.

Passionate, Holistic Health Advocate for Moms & Babies

Hi, I'm Sierra — Certified Breastfeeding Specialist, bioengineer and mom. Feeding your baby can be deeply rewarding, but it can come with challenges. My goal is to help make your feeding experience feel seen, supported, and sustainable.

Premature delivery, NICU struggles, triple-feeding, cranial remolding helmets — oh my! My support is about more than just scientific guidance and clinical outcomes. I've been on all sides of exclusively pumping, exclusively nursing, and combo feeding life (and even a few things in between!). I understand how overwhelming those early days can be, and how important it is to have someone by your side who truly gets what you're going through.

You were meant for motherhood. But you were never meant to do it alone! You are capable and you're doing an incredible job — I'm here to help fill in the gaps and empower you to feed with confidence.

My approach is compassionate, empowering, and rooted in up-to-date, evidence-based care. Whether you're working through latch issues, concerns about supply, planning a return to work, or simply looking for reassurance, I'm here to support you in a way that fits your unique needs.
